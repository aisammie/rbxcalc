This RBX calculator helps game developers calculate accurate earnings after marketplace tax. Perfect for pricing virtual items and calculating profits.

How it works

Roblox charges a 30% marketplace tax on all Robux transactions including:

  • Game pass sales
  • Developer product purchases
  • Asset sales in the catalog
  • Group fund payouts

Tax Mode: Calculate net earnings from gross amount.
Reverse Mode: Calculate required gross amount to achieve desired net earnings.

DevEx (Developer Exchange) allows you to convert Robux to real money:

  • Current rate: ~285 Robux = $1 USD
  • Minimum cashout: 30,000 Robux ($105)
  • Must be Premium member
  • 13+ years old required
  • Valid tax information needed

Note: DevEx rates may change. Always check official Roblox documentation for current rates.
